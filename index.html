Our Story

Crafting Memorable Dining Experiences Since 1995

Founded by Chef Michael Laurent, Gourmet Haven began as a small bistro with a passion for bringing French culinary techniques to local ingredients. What started as a 12-table restaurant has grown into an award-winning dining destination.

Our philosophy is simple: source the best ingredients, treat them with respect, and let their natural flavors shine. We work closely with local farmers and purveyors to ensure every dish tells a story of quality and sustainability.

Whether you're joining us for a special occasion or a casual evening out, we promise an unforgettable culinary journey.
